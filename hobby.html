My Hobbies

Basketball

I started playing basketball in junior high school and continue to this day. At first, I played just for fun, but when I entered junior high, I began competing for my school team.

After graduating, I enrolled in a vocational high school majoring in Software Engineering. While I didn't have time for competitive play during this period, I still enjoyed casual games with friends. Occasionally, I helped train players at my former junior high school.

I believe physical sports are essential for my health, especially since my current job as a web developer involves long hours of sitting with minimal movement. Basketball keeps me active and helps maintain my physical well-being.

Online Gaming

Besides basketball, I enjoy playing various genres of online games, particularly MOBA and RPG games. The only MOBA game I play is Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB). Through this game, I've learned about the importance of team communication, cooperation, and understanding the META (Most Efficient Tactic Available).

I've met many friends through MLBB and eventually formed my own party. We typically play together every weekend night, creating memorable gaming sessions.

However, playing only competitive MOBA games can sometimes be stressful due to the demands of teamwork and keeping up with the META. That's why when I want to relax, I switch to RPG games like Honkai: Star Rail and Genshin Impact. These games offer captivating stories and beautiful music that make the experience enjoyable. While they do have META elements, RPGs provide the option to play casually without competitive pressure, making them the perfect alternative when I need a more relaxed gaming experience.
